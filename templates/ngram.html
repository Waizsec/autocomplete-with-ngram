About N-Gram : N-gram algorithms are commonly used in autocomplete systems to predict the next word or phrase a user is likely to type based on the context of their previous input. By analyzing N-grams (typically bigrams or trigrams) in a large dataset of text, the algorithm identifies frequently occurring sequences of words. When a user begins typing, the algorithm suggests word or phrase completions that match the input context, enhancing the user's typing experience and providing relevant suggestions as they type.
