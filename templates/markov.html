About Markov : The Markov Model is an autocomplete algorithm that uses a statistical approach to predict the next word or token in a text based on the context or history of previous words. This algorithm builds a probabilistic model that represents the likelihood of transitions between words in a specific sequence. When a user enters a series of words, the algorithm identifies the recent history of those words and, based on the probabilistic model, attempts to select the next word that is most likely. This allows the algorithm to provide relevant next-word recommendations, making it valuable for autocomplete features in text processing and other applications that require word predictions based on context.
